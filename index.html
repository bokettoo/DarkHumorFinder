About

Welcome to The Joke's on Us! This website is your destination for dark humor. Whether you're in need of a quick laugh or searching for that perfect joke to lighten the mood, we've got you covered.

Simply enter a keyword related to the type of joke you're looking for, specify the number of jokes you want, and hit "Find Jokes". If you prefer, you can leave the fields blank to get random jokes.

Enjoy the twisted humor, and remember, laughter is the best medicine!
